Understanding Vehicle Values

Each vehicle in Military Tycoon is assigned a value based on several factors:

Demand

How many players are looking to obtain this vehicle. Higher demand typically means higher value.

  • High - Many players want this vehicle
  • Medium - Average interest in this vehicle
  • Low - Few players seeking this vehicle
Scarcity

How rare the vehicle is within the game. Rarer vehicles tend to be more valuable.

  • Common - Easily obtainable
  • Uncommon - Somewhat difficult to obtain
  • Rare - Difficult to obtain
  • Very Rare - Very difficult to obtain
  • Legendary - Extremely rare
Trend

How the vehicle's value is changing over time.

  • Rising - Value is increasing
  • Stable - Value remains consistent
  • Falling - Value is decreasing
Functionality

How useful the vehicle is in gameplay. Rated from 1-10.

  • 8-10 - Highly functional, excellent performance
  • 5-7 - Average functionality and performance
  • 1-4 - Limited functionality or poor performance
